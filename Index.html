Minecraft:

The ultimate "sandbox" game

What is MineCraft? - Minecraft is a sandbox game. A sandbox game is a videogame in which the player is given a great deal of creative ability to work towards certain goals within the game, if there are any. Minecraft is great because it takes that concept to another level, in Minecraft you really can do anything. Wether you're a competative gamer, a casual survival player, or someone looking to express their creativity through this block-based building game, minecraft has a place for you.
